About Me

Hi! I'm David Tench, a fourth-year grad student in the College of Information and Computer Sciences at UMass Amherst. My advisor is Andrew McGregor. Email me at dtench@protonmail.ch.

My interests include complexity theory, streaming algorithms, random graph theory, game theory/mechanism design, and multiagent systems.
